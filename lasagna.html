Classic Italian Lasagna

This lasagna recipe is a hearty and comforting dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese.

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 300ml béchamel sauce
  • 200g mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a pan, cook the chopped onion and minced garlic until soft.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned. Stir in the canned tomatoes and tomato paste. Season with salt and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, followed by a layer of lasagna sheets, then a layer of béchamel sauce. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of béchamel sauce on top.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese on top.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  7. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil.
