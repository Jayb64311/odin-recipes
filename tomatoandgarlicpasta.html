Tomato and Garlic Pasta

Pasta made using tomato sauce and garlic

This is a simple and delicious recipe for tomato and garlic pasta. It requires minimal ingredients and can be prepared in under 30 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Steps:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© the minced garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the canned tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Drain the pasta and mix it with the sauce.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil.
